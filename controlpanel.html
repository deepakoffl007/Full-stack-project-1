Control Panel Design & Manufacturing | UL 508A Listed

To ensure that control panels are ready to use and error-free, CANDID Machines provides fully assembled and tested panels as a UL 508A listed shop.

From Design to Delivery

Our inventive team focuses on error minimization by creating detailed 3D models of each control panel before production begins. This includes wiring layouts, routing schemes, and low-voltage signal separation to minimize interference.

Our engineers design electrical schematics using JIC or IEC standards and configure software parameters to ensure maximum system reliability. All control panels are tested using DMAIC, FMEA, and DFMEA methodologies.

Control Panel Services

  • 2D layouts with tagged wiring and material lists
  • Custom mechanical brackets for mounting
  • Turn-key panel solutions
  • 3D models with color-coded AC/DC routing
  • Compliance with NEC and IEC standards
  • 100% tested and assembled for efficiency
